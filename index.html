How to pick the right LEAP: The deeper ITM you go, the more your LEAP behaves like owning shares (high delta) but costs more. The real hidden cost is extrinsic value — the part of the premium you paid that isn't intrinsic value. This is the true cost of the strategy, because even if the stock stays flat, you lose the extrinsic portion at expiry. Sweet spot: Delta 0.70–0.80, minimal extrinsic.

📉 How Premiums Reduce Your Real Risk
Every CC premium you collect reduces your cost basis in the LEAP. Once total premiums collected = LEAP cost, the LEAP is effectively free — assignment at any price becomes profitable.
